Assessment2

Q1:Describe in your own words, what is design intent? 

(以你的話描述什麼是設計意圖?)

A1:Design Intent is the practice of developing your project’s objectives and  requirements even before working on your design.

(設計意圖是指執行你在設計前要準備的開發項目目的與要求的實踐。)

Q2:What is the difference between sketch entities that are colored blue, black, and            red? 

(藍色，黑色，紅色的草圖實體差異在哪?)

A2:Blue means it is underconstrained, black means it is fully constrained, and red means  it is overconstrained

(藍色是指沒被約束，黑色是指完全約束，紅色是指過度約束。)

Q3:What is automatic inferencing, and how is it utilized? 

(什麼是自動判定，還有該怎麼使用?)

A3:When Onshape automatically assigns constraints to sketch entities while sketching. It can be activated by hovering over an existing entity, or temporarily turned off by
selecting the [shift] key.

(當Onshape在繪製草圖時自動為草圖實體指定約束時。 可以通過將鼠標懸停在現有實體上來激活它，也可以通過選擇[shift]鍵暫時關閉它。)

Q4:Which feature is this and what is it used for : ?

(這是什麼功能還有它用於什麼?)

A4:This is the plane tool, used to create new planes in addition to the default
Top/Right/Front planes.

(這是平面工具，使用於在除了上視/右視/前視圖外，還可以創建平面。)

Q5:How are fillets and chamfers similar? How are they different? 

(圓角和去角相似在哪? 還有差異在哪?)

A5:They are the same in that both features are applied to edges only, they are different in that a fillet creates a round profile, and a chamfer creates a flat profile.

(它們的相同之處在於兩個特徵僅應用於邊緣，它們的不同之處在於圓角創建圓形輪廓，倒角創建平坦輪廓。)
